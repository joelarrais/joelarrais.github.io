About Me

I am a computer scientist with leading research interests in Deep Learning applied to Computational Biology and Bioinformatics.

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Informatics Engeneering, University of Coimbra and a researcher at CISUC. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from University of Aveiro (2010).
